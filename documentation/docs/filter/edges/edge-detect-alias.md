# EdgeDetectAlias TL

**Author:** Tony Lyons - [https://compositingmentor.com](https://compositingmentor.com)

![edge-detect-alias-2.webp](../../img/tools/filter/edges/edge-detect-alias-2.webp)

Analyzes your alpha for aliased edges and gives you an edge detect for aliased areas. You can then use an iBlur or blur with mask to introduce blurs to help smooth out edges. Good for CG or DMP or deep combines where you get some harsh edges.
